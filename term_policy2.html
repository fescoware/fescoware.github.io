PRIVACY POLICY

Thank you for playing our games! This Privacy Policy describes the ways we collect data about you and why we do so. This Privacy Policy applies to Paper Knight Quest games.

THE DATA WE COLLECT

Create account username and password, gameplay data and your interactions with other players inside the Service. This game does not use personal data such as E-Mail, Address or Phone.

WHY DO WE COLLECT YOUR DATA

We collect gameplay data, such as game progress, scores, and in-game transactions, to create a leaderboard and score system and analyze user behavior, improve game mechanics, and also develop new features. The cloud save data also needs an account and password to work properly.

SECURITY

We do not collect any personal information from players, but we collect gameplay data, Thus, we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and I cannot guarantee its absolute security.

IN-APP PURCHASES

Our games enable in-app purchases. Payment transactions for in-app purchases are completed through the app store via your app store account. We do not process payments for in-app purchases, nor do we have access to your credit card information.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

We do not collect any personal information from children, with the sole exception of online identifiers.

CONTACT US

If you have questions about data protection, or if you have any requests for resolving issues with your personal data, we encourage you to primarily contact us through the game so we can reply to you more quickly.
